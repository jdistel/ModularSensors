19  * Accuracy is ± 5 % or 0.3 NTU
20  * Range is 0.1 to 1000 NTU
21  *
22  * For Temperature:
23  * Resolution is 0.1 °C
24  * Accuracy is ± 0.2°C
25  * Range is 0°C to + 50°C
26  *

28  * Time between "StartMeasurement" command and stable reading - 22sec
29  */
