9  *
10  *This file is for the Yosemitech Y4000 MultiParameter Sonde
11  *It is dependent on the YosemitechParent super class.
12  *
13  *Documentation for the Modbus Protocol commands and responses can be found
14  *within the documentation in the YosemitechModbus library at:
15  *https://github.com/EnviroDIY/YosemitechModbus
16  *
17  *These devices output very high "resolution" (32bits) so the resolutions are
18  *based on their accuracy, not the resolution of the sensor.
19  *For details, see documentaition for individual sensor probes
20  *
21  * For Dissolved Oxygen:
22  * Resolution is 0.01 mg/L
23  * Accuracy is ± 0.3 mg/L
24  * Range is 0-20mg/L or 0-200% Air Saturation
25  *
26  * For Turbidity:
27  * Accuracy is ＜5% or 0.3NTU
28  * Range is 0.1~1000 NTU
29  *
30  * For Conductivity:
31  * Resolution is 0.1 µS/cm
32  * Accuracy is ± 1 % Full Scale
33  * Range is 1 µS/cm to 200 mS/cm
34  *
35  * For pH:
36  * Resolution is 0.01 pH units
37  * Accuracy is ± 0.1 pH units
38  * Range is 2 to 12 pH units
39  *
40  * For Temperature:
41  * Resolution is 0.1 °C
42  * Accuracy is ± 0.2°C
43  * Range is 0°C to + 50°C
44  *
45  * For ORP:
46  * Resolution is 1 mV
47  * Accuracy is ± 20 mV
48  * Range is -999 ~ 999 mV
49  *
50  * For Chlorophyll:
51  * Resolution is 0.1 µg/L / 0.1 RFU
52  * Accuracy is ± 1 %
53  * Range is 0 to 400 µg/L or 0 to 100 RFU
54  *
55  * For Blue-Green Algae:
56  * Resolution is 0.01 µg/L / 0.01 RFU
57  * Accuracy is ± 0.04ug/L PC
58  * Range is 0 to 100 µg/L or 0 to 100 RFU
59  *
60  * Time before sensor responds after power - 275-300ms (use 350 for safety)
61  * Time between "StartMeasurement" command and stable reading depends on the
62  * indindividual sensor probes, with Y520 Conductivity taking the longest
63 */
