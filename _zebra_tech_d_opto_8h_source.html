8  *This file is for the ZebraTech D-Opto digital dissolved oxygen sensor
9  *This sensor communicates via SDI-12
10  *
11  *The manual for this sensor is available at:
12  * www.zebra-tech.co.nz/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/D-Opto-Sensor-manual-A4-ver-2.pdf
13  *
14  * For Dissolved Oxygen:
15  * Accuracy is 1% of reading or 0.02PPM, whichever is greater
16  * Resolution is 0.01% / 0.001 PPM
17  *
18  * For Temperature:
19  * Accuracy is ± 0.1°C
20  * Resolution is 0.01°C
21  *
22  * Maximum warm-up time in SDI-12 mode: ~250ms
23  * Excitiation time before measurement: ~5225ms
24  * Maximum measurement duration: ~110ms
25  *
26  * Obnoxiously, the sensor will not take a "concurrent" measurement and leave
27  * the sensor powered on, so we must wait entire ~5200ms exitation time and the
28  * 110ms measurement time each time.
29  * There is the ability to do a non-concurrent measurement and leave the
30  * sensor powered on, in which case the re-measurement takes ~110ms, but doing
31  * it that way the sensor would send an interruption when it was finished,
32  * possibly colliding with and confusing other sensor results
33  *
34  */
